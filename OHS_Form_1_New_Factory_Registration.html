The Factories Act, 1973, Section 9

Section 9 of the Factories Act, 1973, requires that, before any person occupies or uses as a factory any premises which were not occupied or used by him at the commencement of the Act he shall apply for the registration of such premises by sending to the Director of Occupational Health and Safety a written notice containing the particulars set out in the First Schedule of the Act. This form may be used for the purpose. When completed it should be sent to the Director of Occupational Health and Safety, Private Bag 00241, Gaborone.

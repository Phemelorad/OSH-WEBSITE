Scars from injuries or burns which result in disfigurement shall be treated as resulting in from 0 to 50% permanent incapacity according to their size and location.

Total permanent loss of the use of a member shall be treated as loss of such member.

The total loss of a sexual organ or other internal organ shall be treated as resulting in from 0 to 75% of the incapacity depending on the location and effects of such loss.

The percentage of incapacity for ankyloses of any joint shall be reckoned as from 25 to 100% of the incapacity for loss of the part of that joint, according to whether the joint is ankylosed in a favorable or unfavorable position.

Where there is a loss of two or more parts of the hand, the percentage of incapacity shall not be more than for the loss of the whole hand.

Injuries which result in permanent incapacity but which are not included in this schedule shall be assessed in relation to the percentages of incapacity specified in this schedule, whenever possible.
